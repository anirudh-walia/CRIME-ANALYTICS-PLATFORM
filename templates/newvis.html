CRIME VISUALIZATION

Data visualization technology can bring benefits to almost everyone within the police force. The more police are able to see the big picture, the higher the likelihood of crime getting solved quickly. The public increasingly expects this kind of approach. This module showcases data visuals using illustrative animations to depict the patterns of crime in all the states of India for the past 15 years. Demographic factors such as literacy rates & crime per one lakh people have been considered to give effective definition to the visuals per state.
